Pokemon GO and Location Surveillance

Authored By: Patricio Bayas

Did you know that our beloved game Pokémon Go was tracking your every movement and using your location. Pokémon Go has been spying on us. They use your location and then take us on a little adventure outside to nearby specific places and predict if we are gonna be there. This was a large experiment that focused on our behavior and remote control over the game we play. They disguised this as a game that entertained us so we wouldn’t ask questions when poke stops kept popping up at retail locations. They call these "lure modules" and having one at your place of business can attract players of these virtual reality games.

Pokemon go is a virtual reality game where players can access virtual items and locations

Behavioral Stalking

Authored By: Patricio Bayas

Of course we all use google and we love it but we don’t really think about how much data we are giving out. Just to begin with all our emails and all our queries are stored to improve the text prediction algorithms. You may be thinking “oh” I’m not that interesting google isn’t looking at my stuff well the thing is at the end of the day data storage is cheap nowadays and a company like google can afford to store all your data for later use as a matter of fact it’s almost a principle for enterprise systems to have as much data as possible to make better predictions.
